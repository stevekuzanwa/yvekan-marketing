  • JUVKUZ COFFEE

    The JUVKUZ website already had the advantage of having a good domain age and being an established brand name amongst coffee enthusiasts. We introduced new keywords and negative keywords into the account, helping to ensure that the adverts were only showing for targeted searches, whilst also removing unnecessary broad match campaigns. Landing pages were a priority too; ensuring that all ads were pointing to optimal selling pages would help ensure a decrease in bounce rate.
